🎬 HOW TO PLAY

In PunFiction: Box Office Blunders, your goal is to guess a hilarious parody movie title based on its comedic plot and a punned movie quote!

🍿 THE "JAWS" EXAMPLE:

Parody Quote: "You're gonna need a bigger coat!"

Comedic Plot: A giant Great White shark terrorizes a ski resort town by freezing the water and judging people's outerwear.

Guess the Parody Movie: ???

This puzzle involves finding the Original quote and movie these parodies are based on.

Original Movie: Jaws
